Bacon-Wrapped Jalapeño

Prep time:

15 mins

Cook time:

25 mins

Total Time:

40 mins

Servings:

6

Yield:

12 Poppers

creamy in the middle with crispy bacon on the outside.!

a jalapeño popper is a type of stuffed pepper. The pepper in question, naturally is a jalapeño, which has been cored and hollowed out. The interior of the pepper is then filled with a stuffing. In many cases, the filling consists simply of cream cheese.

What do you need?

  • Cream Cheese
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Salt
  • Jalapeno peppers
  • Bacon

Ingredients

  • ½ cup cream cheese
  • ½ cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
  • 12 jalapeno peppers, halved lengthwise, seeds and membranes removed
  • 12 slices bacon

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil.
  2. Mix cream cheese and Cheddar cheese together in a bowl until evenly blended. Fill each jalapeño half with cheese mixture. Put halves back together and wrap each stuffed pepper with a slice of bacon. Arrange bacon-wrapped peppers on the prepared baking sheet.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until bacon is crispy, 25 to 35 minutes.
